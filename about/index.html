About the ClinEpi Wiki

About this project

This website and project was designed for a few reasons:

  1. To improve access to education in Evidence Based Medicine and Clinical Epidemiology
  2. To provide an easily navigatable directory for biostatistics information
  3. As a way for me to personally organise my thoughts around clinical epidemiology and biostatistics, and record things that I have learnt
  4. To act as a portfolio in Clin Epi teaching
  5. As a place to organise information for the EBM-based podcast
